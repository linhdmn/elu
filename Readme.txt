------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Discription:
Set the work time and short break time to keep users's eyes stay healthy.
Include 2 functions:
Set the notification for work time in 4 choices: 30 mins, 40 mins, 50 mins and 60 mins 
on the left radio button column.
Set the notification for short break time in 4 choice: 5 mins, 10 mins, 15 mins and 20 mins 
on the right radio button column.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Main flow:
Click to choose work time on the left and choose the short break on the right.
Then click on "Start" button to start.
If you want to stop it, click "Stop" button to stop the counting notification time.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Additon flow:
If there is no choice for both work time and short break, clicking the "Start" button will show
the exceptions handle message "Vui long chon thoi gian"
